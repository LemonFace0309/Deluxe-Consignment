About Us

Based out of Toronto, Canada, Deluxe Consignment is an online consignment shop consisting of a team of highly dedicated and passionate individuals that are seeking to connect you with the best deals on new and pre-loved merchandise. At DC, we strive to continuously improve our business and services to meet our growing demands. We pride ourselves in offering authentic, affordable, and competitively priced items that meets the expectation of both buyers and consignors. Maintaining an online presence, and establishing ourselves as an online retailer enables us to lower our overhead fees, and offer low consignment fees to our consignors, while at the same time pricing each item at very affordable rates for buyers.  

 

 

Out Authenticity Guarantee

Each item we receive is individually authenticated in house or through external authenticators. We use three authenticators interchangeably depending on the brands we receive in. As of recent, we've also become one of the first Canadian consignment shops to use Entrupy, a devise that authenticates on a microscopic level through ultrasound scanning of each item. It's safe to say that we take authentication very, very seriously! 

 

 

All of our items are carefully processed, authenticated, and then photographed prior to being listed for sale. We are confident in our authenticity process and offer a 100% authentic or money back guarantee on all purchases. We also welcome you to consult external authenticators, and are always happen to send you additional photos if necessary. For more information on how we authenticate, or the authenticators we use, please email us at support@deluxeconsignmentshop.com. Happy Shopping!
