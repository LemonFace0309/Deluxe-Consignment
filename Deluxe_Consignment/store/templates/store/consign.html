We at DeLuxe Consignment are always interested in helping you re-home your brand new or pre-loved designer items! At the moment, we are accepting items with the minimum resale value of $125 and above. We typically accept handbags, accessories, jewelry, and brand new or lightly-worn shoes and outerwear (if season permits). 

 

Our fees vary based on quoted selling price of item, and ranges from 15% up to a flat fee of $450 plus Paypal processing fees of 3%. A typical consignment period is 60-days, after which renewal is optional if storage capacity permits. 

 

If you are interested in consigning with us, and would like to obtain more information, please contact us at: deluxe.consignment@gmail.com, or fill in our form here (Google account required). In your email, please include the following information: 

 

  • Name of product: be as specific as possible. 
  • Description of item condition: new or used, signs of wear, etc. 
  • Photos of the actual item: not stock photos. 
  • What the item comes with: e.g., dustbag, authenticity card, tags, etc
  • Year and location of purchase, if possible.
